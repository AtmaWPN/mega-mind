How to Play
click "START" to begin the game with current settings
click "SETTINGS" to open the settings menu
navigate the settings menu with the arrow keys
when finished changing the settings,
click "BACK" or navigate to "BACK" and press enter
INGAME
the ingame controls are as follows:
up/down arrows: move the color selection cursor
left/right arrows: move the guess placement cursor
enter: place the selected color into the selected location
spacebar: make a guess
the mouse can also be used to select colors and locations
